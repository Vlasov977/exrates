How to submit a coin for listing

Exrates is a fast-growing crypto exchange. Every day more than 57 000 traders buy and sell 94 coins worth $42,467,000. The trading volumes, as well as the number of traders, are growing day by day. Listed coins at Exrates get huge privileges: full maintenance, extra three trading pairings, commission and informational support via all our channels. 01

Exrates will review each application
within 24 hours, even if project only
prepares for ICO.

02

After the approval of the application, ICO should provide technical data about the coin. You can find all the preconditions in the “Technical Requirements” section.

03

Listing Department checks technical information about the coin.

04

Two parties sign agreement. Then comes the official announcement of future listing.

05

The Exrates team integrates the coin to the exchange. You can read more about terms of setup in “Technical Requirements” section.

06

Exrates announces new listing via social media. Traders buy and sell your coin, pumping up the trading volumes.
